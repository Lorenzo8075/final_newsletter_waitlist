OPT Premium Processing

USCIS is now offering premium processing for F-1 students who are applying for OPT or STEM OPT extensions. This means that you can get a decision on your application within 30 days, for a fee of $1,500. Previously, it could take several months to get a decision on these applications, so this is a big improvement.


To be eligible for premium processing, you must:

  • Be an F-1 student
  • Be applying for OPT or STEM OPT extension
  • Have a pending Form I-765 application

You can file for premium processing by:

  • Mailing Form I-907 with your Form I-765
  • Filing Form I-907 electronically through your MyUSCIS account
